Sun Set Time

Arrive at the lake a little before the calculated sunset time to ensure you have enough time to find a good spot, set up any equipment, and settle in for the view.

You may want to bring a blanket, some snacks, and a camera to capture the moment. The best sunset colors typically appear 10-15 minutes after the sun dips below the horizon. Check local weather conditions as clouds can enhance or diminish the viewing experience.
